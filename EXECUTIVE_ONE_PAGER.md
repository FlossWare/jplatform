## The Solution: Apartment Building vs. Single-Family Homes

**Traditional Containers**: Each app gets its own house (full OS, isolated network)  
**Platform-Java**: All apps share one building (one JVM) but get private apartments (isolated classloaders)

### What You Get

✅ **75% lower infrastructure costs** ($13K vs. $52K annually for 100 apps)  
✅ **15x faster deployments** (30 seconds vs. 8 minutes)  
✅ **Zero-downtime updates** (hot-reload without restart)  
✅ **Simplified security** (one OS to patch, not 100+ container images)  
✅ **True isolation** (ClassLoader, threads, security policies, resource limits per app)
